## Features/Information

- Goal:
    - Loop all stages
- Mods do not work on the beta, so make sure to back up your beta save if you went into the beta
- Controller navigation is broken in character/stage selection
    - cry about it, too lazy to figure out how to fix
- DeathLink
  - can be enabled/disabled on the main menu
  - pausing can block deathlinks (this is to prevent crashes, please do not abuse)
  - you can not receive a deathlink within 4s of another
- QoL Mod (AutoUnexcludeEquippedWeapons)
  - this is a QoL mod included with the mod 
  - if you have a sealed/banished weapon it will be unsealed/unbanished for the run so it can be leveled up


## Commonly Asked Questions

- Why Didn't I Start With Characters?
  - You can only see characters you've unlocked in vanilla.
  - Either limit yourself to characters you have unlocked when adjusting your yaml, or unlock characters first, either vanilla or by using the secret menu (Vampire Survivors Wiki).

- Are Weapons And Passives Randomized In?
  - No, not yet or for the forseeable future.

- Why Didn't My Stage Check Send?
  - Stages actually end past the time that they say they do, meaning you need endless to send the checks.
  - You are also required to have Hurry to complete stages and enemy checks past 15 minutes currently.
