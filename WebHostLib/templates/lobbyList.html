Game Lobbies

Lobbies let you gather a group before generating a multiworld. Instead of collecting everyone's YAML files yourself, create a lobby and share the link - each player joins, uploads their own config, and the host generates the seed once everyone is ready. Lobbies include a chat so you can coordinate settings and sort out any issues before rolling. Depending on the host's choice, custom worlds not hosted on this website will be allowed or not.
